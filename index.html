How To Simplify Your Life

Living a life where you're happier and kinder towards yourself is not just about what you can do. But also about what you are already doing. And especially those habits that do have a negative and unhappy impact on your life.

10 Keys to Happier Living

Give sincere compliments often. You can easily lift someone's spirits by complimenting their work, attitude, style, or anything else you genuinely find remarkable. Not only will it help them but it will help you be seen as a person who is uplifiting and nice to have around.

10 Things You Need to Stop Doing to Start Living a Happier Life

When one door of happiness closes, another opens, but often we look so long at the closed door that we do not see one that has been opened for us.

Positive Reminders

All situations are temporary. Nothing lasts forever. Buildings collapse, plants die, hit cups of coffee go cold, tires go flat. Everything is always in transition. Here is the key: even when your coffee gets cold, it's still a coffee you want and you can find a way to enjoy it.

Everything comes at the right time

Happiness is a habit that we have to practice on a daily basis. Life is full of uncertainty, fear, and struggle, but we can cultivate a practice that brings out the true joy living inside us at all times.

"Happiness is when what you think, what you say, and what you do are in harmony"

Love yourself. Ask yourself a question: would you let someone you deeply love feel bad, stressed and overwhelmed? Probably not. You would do whatever you can to help them. Why is it so hard for us to love ourselves in the same way? There is nothing in the world more important than loving yourself.
